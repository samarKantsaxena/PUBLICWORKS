We Fix Neighbourhoods, Together

PUBLICWORKS is a citizen‑led platform that transforms complaints into action.

Our Story

Every day, millions of people walk past broken streetlights, potholes that damage vehicles, garbage heaps that attract disease, and leaking pipes that waste precious water. They take a photo, post it on social media, and hope someone sees it. Usually, no one does. PUBLICWORKS was born to change that — to give every citizen a direct, transparent, and accountable channel to report civic issues and track them until they are fixed.

What started as a small experiment in one neighbourhood quickly revealed a universal truth: people care deeply about their surroundings, but the systems to report problems are broken. Municipal helplines are understaffed, email complaints vanish, and WhatsApp messages get lost. PUBLICWORKS bridges that gap by combining simple reporting with AI-powered categorisation, real‑time maps, and a unique partnership with local NGOs and authorities who can claim issues and resolve them.

How It Works

Citizens sign up for free, snap a photo of any civic issue, and pin the location on our map. Our AI automatically classifies the problem (pothole, garbage, drainage, etc.) and suggests a severity level. The issue appears on the public map for everyone to see, and community members can upvote issues that matter most to them. Meanwhile, registered NGOs and local government bodies can claim issues they are equipped to solve, update the status, and add comments. When the problem is fixed, the reporter gets a notification, and the “resolved” marker becomes a badge of progress.

We also believe in radical transparency. Every issue has its own public page with a unique link — shareable on social media, email, or WhatsApp. This puts social pressure on responsible authorities and gives NGOs the visibility they deserve. Over time, PUBLICWORKS builds a powerful dataset of maintenance patterns, helping cities plan better and allocate resources where they are needed most.

Why PUBLICWORKS?

Unlike other reporting apps that stop at “submit”, PUBLICWORKS closes the loop. We don't just collect complaints – we actively facilitate resolution by connecting citizens with verified NGOs and authorities. Our AI helps prioritise urgent issues (e.g., a deep pothole on a school route vs. a faded zebra crossing). And because our platform is free and ad‑light, we remain focused on public welfare, not profit.

Today, PUBLICWORKS is a community of thousands of citizens, dozens of NGOs, and a growing number of municipal partners. Every fixed pothole, every repaired light, every cleared drain is a small victory for collective action. We invite you to join us – report an issue, claim one as an NGO, or simply spread the word. Together, we can make public works truly work for the public.
